What It Does

An Operating System for Your Life

Autonomous Agent

Ticks continuously in the background. Reads your goals, your tasks, your environment — and decides what to do next without being asked.

You're Always in Control

PC Brain never acts without boundaries. Every action the agent can take is controlled by simple rules YOU set — written in plain English, not code. Change what your AI can and can't do in seconds.

Mission Control Dashboard

A real-time command center across 7 tabs and 28+ live tiles. Devices, finances, goals, smart home, and agent thoughts — all connected.

Multi-Provider AI

Switch between Claude, GPT-4o, and Gemini in 30 seconds from Settings. No restart. Your agent, your choice of intelligence.

Completely Private

PC Brain disappears when you close it. No background processes phoning home. No data sitting on someone else's server. When you walk away, it's like it was never there.

Distributed Intelligence

Delegate tasks to worker nodes on other machines over a Tailscale mesh. PC Brain scales across every device you own.

PC Brain operates under safe default rules from the moment you install it. It will never delete files, send messages, or make purchases without your explicit approval. Every boundary is yours to adjust — as simply as editing a list.
Under the Hood

Built Different by Design

OBSERVE

The agent reads your goals, active tasks, smart home state, finances, and episodic memory on every tick.

DECIDE

A governed reasoning chain — OBSERVE, PRIORITIZE, HYPOTHESIZE, GOVERN, DECIDE — produces structured JSON actions.

EXECUTE

Approved actions run. Files move. Tasks update. Devices respond. Everything is logged to your local database.

Privacy First

Your Data. Your Machine. Full Stop.

The #1 question we get: "Is this spying on me?" No. Here's why.

Runs Locally

Your AI lives on your machine and nowhere else. No background syncing, no telemetry by default. Your goals, tasks, and memories are yours alone.

Optional Cloud

The only data that touches our servers: your license key activation (one-time) and optional anonymous version telemetry (opt-in, off by default).

Legal Compliance

GDPR and CCPA compliant by design. We cannot sell your data because we do not have your data.
