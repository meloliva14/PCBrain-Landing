Accessibility Statement

Effective July 4, 2026 | PC Brain LLC, New Jersey | pcbrain.ai/accessibility

Our commitment

PC Brain is built by a small, independent team that believes software you own should also be software you can use — whatever your abilities, whatever assistive technology you rely on. We design and test against the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, Level AA for this website, and we apply the same standard to the PC Brain desktop application's interface as it evolves.

What we do on this site

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper landmarks, heading order, and a skip-to-content link on every page that needs one.
  • Full keyboard operability — every interactive element can be reached and activated without a mouse, with visible focus indicators.
  • Respect for reduced-motion preferences: if your system asks for less motion, our animations, starfields, and scroll effects switch off and the content stands on its own.
  • Labeled form fields with appropriate autocomplete attributes, and no information conveyed by color alone.
  • Decorative visuals marked as such so screen readers skip the scenery and read the substance.

The desktop application

PC Brain runs locally on your Windows machine. We are progressively applying the same accessibility standard across its dashboard and controls, including keyboard navigation and reduced-motion support. Accessibility work on the application is ongoing, and we treat it as a release requirement rather than an afterthought.

Known limitations

This site uses rich animated visuals on the landing page. They are fully disabled under a reduced-motion preference, but if you encounter a combination of browser and assistive technology where anything is hard to perceive or operate, that is a bug to us — please tell us.

Feedback and contact

If anything on this site or in PC Brain is in your way, contact us at melo@pcbrain.ai with “Accessibility” in the subject line. A human reads every message, and accessibility reports are prioritized. We aim to respond within a few business days.

Your data rights

Accessibility and control go together. Details on exporting or deleting your data are in our Privacy Policy.
